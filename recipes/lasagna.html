Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for lasagna. Lasagna is an italian dish getting its name from the sheet-like pasta used in the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Pasta sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tomato paste
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Parsley
  • Egg
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer and bake
  5. Let rest for 15 mins
  6. Remove from oven
  7. Serve with Garlic Bread
