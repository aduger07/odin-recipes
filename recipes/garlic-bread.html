Garlic Bread

Description

Garlic bread is a classic side dish and perfect with everything from Lasagna to a juicy steak dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf french bread
  • 1/2 cup salted butter softened
  • 3 tbs olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 tbs parsley finely chopped
  • 3 tbs grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F
  2. Combine butter, olive oil, garlic and parsley until fluffy
  3. Halve the bread lengthwise
  4. Spread each side of the bread with garlic butter. Sprinkle with cheese if using
  5. Bake 8-10 minutes or until golden and toasted.
